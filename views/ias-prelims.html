What is Civil Service Examination

Civil Services Examination is a competitive examination through which government of India recruits candidates to All India Services (IAS, IPS, IFos), Central Services (IRS, IRTS, ICAS, ADAS, IAAS, IIS and others)

It is conducted in two phases â€“ a preliminary examination consisting of two objective-type papers (general studies and aptitude test), and a main examination consisting of nine papers of subjective type, followed by a personality test (interview).
